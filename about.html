WHO ARE PASCAL IMITATION JEWELLERY?

Statement jewellery and accessories for queens and rebels that refuse to fit in. Pascal Imitation Jewelleryis a Pakistan based jewellery and accessory brand that fuses luxurious exclusivity with swag at affordable prices. The brand embodies br empowerment, individuality in a world full of sheep and dares you to follow your own set of rules! We see you. Our mission is to make jewellery and accessories accessible, fun and fly. Our aim is to ensure you look fresh to death. Be confident. We encourage you to be who you want to be, unapologetically.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Founded in 2016. Designed in Pakistan. Inspired by the world and designed for all. (Hello) graceful, awkward, young, old, fashionista, plain Jane, glam, tom-boy, gym bunny, go-getter and wonder woman. We do this for you. Our philosophy’s pretty simple: go hard or go home!
